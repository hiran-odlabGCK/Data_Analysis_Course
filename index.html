DATA ANALYSIS MICRO-COURSE

In this concise and practical micro course, you will learn the fundamental concepts and techniques of data analysis. Discover how to collect, clean, and transform data into actionable insights using popular tools like Python. Dive into statistical analysis, data visualization, and exploratory data analysis to uncover patterns, trends, and correlations. Gain hands-on experience by working on real-world datasets, applying your newfound knowledge to solve relevant problems. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional seeking to upskill, this micro course will equip you with the essential skills to extract valuable knowledge from data and make informed decisions.
